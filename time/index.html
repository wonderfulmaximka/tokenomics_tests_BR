INSIGHTS
▲
95.7% Year-1 Supply Inflation — Extreme Dilution Risk — Supply nearly doubles in 4 quarters
▾
Circulating supply grows from 227.8M at TGE to 445.7M by Q4 — a 95.7% increase in one year. Driven by Seed unlocking ~20M/quarter, KOL releasing ~4.2M/quarter, Pre-Sale completing by Q7, and Community releasing 18–22M/quarter. Sustaining price under this supply expansion requires extraordinary demand growth. Active demand-side management — staking lockups, fee burns, and real product adoption — is essential to offset structural inflation pressure in year one.
▲
KOL Sale: 49.1% Unlocked at TGE — $9.9M Day-1 Sell Risk — 44.2M tokens liquid immediately
▾
The KOL allocation (90M tokens, 9%) begins with 44.17M unlocked at TGE — 49.1% of the full KOL bucket on day one. With a propensity to sell of 0.901 (driven by the 66/100 utility score), this implies 39.8M tokens (~$9.95M) entering the market on launch day from influencers. A cliff-then-linear structure would be far more appropriate for this cohort. Combined with Seed, total day-one insider sell pressure is estimated at ~$13.4M, representing significant opening-day price risk.
▲
Seed: No Cliff, 20% at TGE — $5.5M Immediate Sell Pressure — 30M unlocked day one, linear through Q7
▾
Seed investors (150M tokens, 15%) receive 30M tokens at TGE with no cliff period. With propensity to sell at 0.736, estimated sell volume is 22.1M tokens = $5.52M on day one. Industry standard requires a 6–12 month cliff before any unlock begins. The absence of a cliff removes the holding incentive for seed investors to stay supportive post-launch. Combined with a fast linear schedule completing by Q7 (under 2 years), this is a structurally weak vesting design for the Seed bucket.
▲
Whitepaper Allocations Don't Match Spreadsheet — Material discrepancy across documents
▾
The whitepaper states: Seed 20%, Pre-Sale 4%, KOL 5%, Advisors 10%, Team 10%. The finalized spreadsheet shows: Seed 15%, Pre-Sale 10%, KOL 9%, Advisors 7.5%, Team 7.5%. Pre-Sale and KOL are more than double what the whitepaper shows. Investors relying solely on the whitepaper are reading materially inaccurate terms. The whitepaper must be updated immediately to reflect final tokenomics, with version control applied to all public documents.
■
$250M FDV at $0.25 Listing — Aggressive vs. Peers — No revenue data to support valuation
▾
At the confirmed listing price of $0.25, FDV = $250M with TGE market cap of $56.9M. For a pre-revenue Web3 social/creator economy project, this is aggressive relative to peers — Friend.tech peaked at ~$50–80M FDV, Stars Arena below $30M. Without confirmed provider contracts, exchange listings, or active users, a $250M FDV requires exceptionally strong celebrity-driven narrative and launch momentum to sustain post-TGE. The project needs to clearly articulate the valuation basis in its investor materials.
■
No Staking APY or Burn Rate Disclosed — Demand-side mechanics absent from documentation
▾
The whitepaper mentions "multiple staking tiers with variable APY" but provides no specifics: no APY rates, lockup durations, or minimum stake amounts. No burn mechanism or fee routing is described. Without these figures, it is impossible to model whether demand-side mechanics can offset the 95.7% year-1 supply inflation. Publishing concrete staking parameters before TGE is critical for exchange due diligence and community confidence.
■
Liquidity Lock Duration Unconfirmed — 90M tokens fully liquid at TGE, no lock published
▾
The full 90M Liquidity allocation (9%) is available from Q1. No lock duration, custodian, or wallet address has been published. Without a confirmed LP token lock (minimum 12 months), on-chain analytics tools will flag rug-pull risk, deterring institutional participation and CEX listing applications. A public lock via PinkLock or Team Finance, with transaction hash, should be announced at or before TGE.
✓
Team Has Clean 4-Quarter Cliff — Strong Alignment Signal — Zero tokens at TGE, ~4-year effective vesting
▾
Team tokens (75M, 7.5%) have a full 1-year cliff with zero tokens at TGE, followed by linear unlock through Q20 — an effective 4-year schedule. Advisors (75M, 7.5%) carry a 3-quarter cliff starting Q4. These are institutional-grade structures that align team and advisor incentives with long-term success. This is the strongest element in the $TIME tokenomics design and signals the core team is not positioned for an early exit.
✓
Multi-Product Ecosystem Creates Genuine Token Demand — $TIME mandatory across 3 products
▾
The Time of Heroes, MEEET, and TIME Network all use $TIME as their exclusive payment currency — creating real, non-optional demand tied to product usage. If celebrity partnerships (Andrew Tate, Gary Vee, Joe Rogan) drive even modest adoption at launch, token velocity could partially offset supply inflation. The multi-product utility architecture is meaningfully stronger than single-use tokens in the SocialFi category.
