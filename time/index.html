INSIGHTS
▲
95.7% Year-1 Supply Inflation — Extreme Dilution Risk — Supply nearly doubles in 4 quarters
▾
Circulating supply grows from 227.8M at TGE to 445.7M by Q4 — a 95.7% increase in one year. Driven by Seed unlocking 20M/quarter, KOL releasing 4.2M/quarter, Pre-Sale completing by Q7, and Community releasing 18–22M/quarter. Sustaining price under this supply expansion requires extraordinary demand growth. Active demand-side management (staking, burning, product adoption) is essential to offset this structural inflation pressure in year one.
▲
KOL Sale: 49.1% Unlocked at TGE — $9.9M Day-1 Sell Risk — 44.2M tokens liquid immediately
▾
The KOL allocation (90M tokens, 9%) begins with 44.17M unlocked at TGE — 49.1% of the full KOL bucket on day one. With a propensity to sell of 0.901 (driven by the 66/100 utility score), this implies 39.8M tokens, or ~$9.95M, entering the market immediately from KOLs. Influencers with high-ROI positions typically sell aggressively at launch to lock in gains. A cliff-then-linear structure would be far more appropriate for this cohort. Combined with Seed, total day-one insider sell pressure reaches ~$13.4M.
▲
Seed: No Cliff, 20% at TGE — $5.5M Immediate Sell Pressure — 30M unlocked day one, linear through Q7
▾
Seed investors (150M tokens, 15%) receive 30M tokens at TGE — a 20% immediate unlock with no cliff. With propensity to sell at 0.736, estimated sell volume is 22.1M tokens = $5.52M on day one. Industry standard for seed investors requires a 6–12 month cliff before any unlock begins. The absence of a cliff removes the lock-up incentive for seed investors to support the token post-launch. Combined with the fast linear schedule (fully unlocked by Q7, under 2 years), this represents a structurally weak vesting design for the Seed bucket.
▲
Whitepaper Allocations Don't Match Spreadsheet — Material discrepancy across documents
▾
The whitepaper states: Seed 20%, Pre-Sale 4%, KOL 5%, Advisors 10%, Team 10%. The finalized tokenomics spreadsheet shows: Seed 15%, Pre-Sale 10%, KOL 9%, Advisors 7.5%, Team 7.5%. These are material differences — Pre-Sale and KOL are more than double what's in the whitepaper. Investors who rely solely on the whitepaper are reading materially inaccurate terms. The whitepaper must be updated immediately to reflect final tokenomics, and version control applied to all public documentation.
■
Listing Price & FDV Undisclosed — No Valuation Anchor — Cannot benchmark against peers
▾
No official listing price or FDV has been disclosed. At the assumed listing price of $0.25, FDV = $250M and initial market cap = $56.9M. For a pre-revenue Web3 social/creator economy project, $250M FDV is aggressive relative to peers — Friend.tech peaked at ~$50–80M FDV, Stars Arena below $30M. Without confirmed traction or revenue, a $250M FDV requires exceptionally strong narrative execution and celebrity-driven demand to sustain post-TGE.
■
No Staking APY or Burn Rate Disclosed — Demand-side mechanics absent
▾
The whitepaper mentions "multiple staking tiers with variable APY" but provides no specifics: no APY rates, lockup durations, or minimum stake amounts. No burn mechanism or fee routing is described. Without these figures, it is impossible to model whether demand-side mechanics can offset the 95.7% year-1 supply inflation. Publishing concrete staking parameters before TGE is critical for exchange due diligence and community confidence.
■
Liquidity Lock Duration Unconfirmed — 90M tokens fully liquid at TGE, no lock confirmed
▾
The full 90M Liquidity allocation (9%) is available from Q1 — appropriate for market making, but no lock duration, custodian, or wallet address has been published. Without a confirmed LP token lock (minimum 12 months recommended), on-chain analytics tools will flag the project as a rug-pull risk, deterring institutional participation and CEX listing applications. A public lock via PinkLock or Team Finance, with the transaction hash shared, should be announced at or before TGE.
✓
Team Has Clean 4-Quarter Cliff — Strong Alignment Signal — Zero tokens at TGE, 4-year effective vesting
▾
Team tokens (75M, 7.5%) have a full 1-year (4-quarter) cliff with zero tokens at TGE, followed by linear unlock through Q20 — an effective 4-year vesting schedule. Advisors (75M, 7.5%) have a 3-quarter cliff starting Q4. These are institutional-grade vesting structures that directly align team and advisor incentives with long-term ecosystem success. This is one of the strongest elements in the $TIME tokenomics design and signals the core team is not positioned for an early exit.
✓
Multi-Product Ecosystem Creates Genuine Token Demand — $TIME mandatory across 3 products
▾
The Time of Heroes, MEEET, and TIME Network all use $TIME as their exclusive payment currency — creating real, non-optional demand tied to product usage. Booking celebrity sessions, purchasing NFT tier passes, paying platform fees, and accessing premium content all require tokens. If celebrity partnerships (Andrew Tate, Gary Vee, Joe Rogan) drive even modest adoption at launch, token velocity could partially offset supply inflation. The multi-product utility architecture is meaningfully stronger than single-use tokens.
